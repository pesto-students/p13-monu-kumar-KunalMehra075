Lata Mangeshkar Fan Webpage

Lata Mangeshkar is an Indian playback singer and one of the most renowned and respected voices in the Indian music industry. She was born on September 28, 1929, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. Lata Mangeshkar has recorded songs in various Indian languages, including Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Assamese, Gujarati, Kannada, Bhojpuri, Malayalam, Punjabi, and Urdu.
She started her singing career in 1942 at the age of 13 and has since then recorded thousands of songs in her career spanning over seven decades. Lata Mangeshkar is known for her melodious and versatile voice and has received numerous accolades and awards for her contributions to Indian music, including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
